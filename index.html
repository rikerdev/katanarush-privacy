Privacy Policy

This privacy policy is applicable to the Katana Rush app (hereinafter referred to as "Application") for mobile devices, which was developed by Rikerdev (hereinafter referred to as "Service Provider") as an Ad Supported service with optional in-app purchases. This service is provided "AS IS".

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

The Application does not require you to create an account or provide personal information directly. Sign-in with Google Play Games is optional and used only to enable leaderboards, achievements, and cloud save.

What information does the Application collect automatically?

The Application may collect the following information automatically:

  • Type of mobile device, model and manufacturer
  • Mobile device's unique identifiers (including Android Advertising ID / AAID)
  • IP address and approximate location derived from it (country/region level)
  • Mobile operating system and version
  • Information about how you use the Application (session length, level progress, in-game events, crash reports, performance metrics)
  • Purchase history metadata (which items you bought — payment details are processed by Google and never received by the Service Provider)

Does the Application collect precise real time location information of the device?

No. This Application does not gather precise (GPS) location. Only coarse region-level location derived from IP address by advertising partners.

Does the Application use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies?

No. The Application does not use AI technologies to process your data or provide features.

Advertising

The Application displays advertisements provided by third-party ad networks through Unity LevelPlay (ironSource) mediation, including Unity Ads. These networks may use the Android Advertising ID (AAID), IP address, and ad interaction data to deliver personalized or contextual advertising and to measure ad performance. You can reset your Advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads at any time in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads).

In-App Purchases

The Application offers optional in-app purchases processed through Google Play Billing. The Service Provider receives only anonymized transaction metadata (product ID, purchase token). Payment information (card details, billing address) is handled entirely by Google and is never accessible to the Service Provider.

Firebase (Google)

The Application uses Firebase services provided by Google to monitor stability, understand aggregate usage, and deliver remote configuration:

  • Firebase Analytics — collects anonymized app event data (gameplay actions, screen views, session duration), device characteristics (model, OS version, language), and a pseudonymous Firebase Installation ID. This data is used to understand aggregate usage patterns and improve the game. It is not linked to your identity.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — collects crash reports, stack traces, device state, and app version at the moment of a crash. This data helps diagnose and fix stability issues.
  • Firebase Remote Config — delivers configuration values to the Application. No user data is sent outbound through this service.

Firebase does not collect your name, email, precise location, or other directly identifying information through this Application. You can review Google's data handling practices at firebase.google.com/support/privacy and policies.google.com/privacy.

Google Play Games Services and Cloud Save

If you sign in with Google Play Games, the Application accesses your public Google Play Games profile (player ID, display name, avatar) to enable leaderboards and achievements. Your game progress may be stored in the private "AppData" folder of your personal Google Drive through Google Play Games Saved Games. This data is owned and controlled by Google and is accessible only to you and this Application. The Service Provider does not operate its own backend or store your save data on its own servers.

What are my opt-out rights?

  • Stop all data collection: Uninstall the Application using the standard uninstall process of your device.
  • Opt out of personalized advertising: In your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads), reset your Advertising ID or enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization".
  • Disconnect Google Play Games: Sign out of Google Play Games in the Google Play Games app to stop leaderboard/achievement syncing.

Data Retention Policy

The Service Provider does not operate its own servers and does not retain personal data independently. Game progress stored in Google Play Games Saved Games remains in your Google account until you delete it.
